About Me

Paul is a self-taught front-end web developer/graphic designer, who fell in love with web development and has strong passion for design. He spends most of his time researching and fine tuning his craft, both code and design to help give his clients the best desired results. Not only is he a web designer but in his spare time he’s also an expiring comic book artist and musician.

“According to a study done by Nielsen, 85 percent of consumers use the internet to find a local business, so a website is even more important if you are a local business” (Source). My job is to help you solve this problem by building you a clean professional website to improve customer service, generate leads, improve credibility, target larger markets, attract new audiences and most importantly customer convenience.
